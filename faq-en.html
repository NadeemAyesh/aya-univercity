Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Gaza Reconstruction Competition

What is the competition and its objectives?

The competition is a global challenge aiming to develop smart geographic solutions using GIS and AI technologies to support reconstruction efforts and emergency rapid intervention in the Gaza Strip.

How can I register for the competition?

You can register through the competition website by clicking the "Register Now" button available on the home page.

What are the competition dates?

The competition starts on Saturday, January 17, 2026. The final deadline for project submission is Saturday, February 21, 2026.
