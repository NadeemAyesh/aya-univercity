Smart Solutions for Reconstruction Efforts in Gaza
Using Modern Geospatial Technologies

The Department of Geography and GIS at the Islamic University of Gaza, in partnership with Skipperfly, announces a global challenge to develop smart geospatial solutions contributing to reconstruction and sustainable recovery in the Gaza Strip.

Our Vision

In light of the challenges facing Gaza, we are moving from theoretical studies to applicable digital solutions. This competition aims to innovate technical tools that support reconstruction and sustainable recovery.
It is a call to creators and technologists worldwide to build smart and sustainable tools that make a real difference in people's lives, turning data into decisions and technology into reconstruction based on sustainability and future resilience.

Our Programs

Debris Management

Smart solutions for assessing and processing war remains

Emergency Housing Planning

Effective modeling of temporary housing (caravans)

Infrastructure Revival

Rehabilitation of vital networks

Camp Management

Smart solutions to facilitate access to relief services

Food & Water Security

Smart models for tracking agriculture and water via remote sensing

Health Sector

Rehabilitation of vital health networks
